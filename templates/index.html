Olivia Bennet

She gained national recognition for her work as an environmental lawyer before entering politics. Her signature policy proposals include aggressive climate action plans, renewable energy investments, and strict environmental regulations to combat pollution. Senator Bennett is known for her eloquent speeches and unwavering commitment to preserving the planet for future generations.

Samuel Ramirez

Samuel represents the Coalition for Economic Prosperity

Samuel Ramirez

Samuel Ramirez is a shrewd and pragmatic leader with a background in economics. He served as the mayor of a major city before becoming a governor. Governor Ramirez is dedicated to revitalizing the economy through deregulation, tax incentives for businesses, and infrastructure development. He's often seen as a proponent of free-market policies and is known for his ability to strike compromises between opposing political factions.

Maya Patel

United Progress Coalition

Maya Patel

Maya Patel is a young and dynamic congresswoman with a background in public health and social justice advocacy. She rose to prominence through community organizing efforts and has a strong commitment to improving healthcare access and affordability. Congresswoman Patel is also a vocal advocate for racial and gender equality, and she champions policies aimed at reducing income inequality and expanding educational opportunities for underprivileged communities.
